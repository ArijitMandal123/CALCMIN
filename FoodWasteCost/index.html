Frequently Asked Questions

How much money does the average family waste on food?

The average American family wastes $1,500-$2,500 annually on food, representing 30-40% of their grocery budget. This equals $4-$7 per day in wasted food.

What foods are wasted most frequently?

Fresh fruits and vegetables account for the highest waste rates (25-30%), followed by dairy products, bread, and leftovers. These categories represent 60-70% of total food waste.
