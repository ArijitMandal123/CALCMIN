What is Crypto Tax Loss Harvesting?

Tax loss harvesting involves selling cryptocurrency holdings at a loss to offset capital gains from other investments. This strategy can reduce your overall tax liability while allowing you to maintain similar market exposure by purchasing similar (but not identical) assets.
