What is a College Major ROI Calculator?

A college major ROI (Return on Investment) calculator is an essential financial planning tool that helps students, parents, and education professionals evaluate the economic value of different college degrees. This powerful calculator analyzes tuition costs, living expenses, opportunity costs, starting salaries, career growth potential, and employment rates to determine which college majors offer the best financial returns over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's a good ROI for a college degree?

A good college ROI is typically 8-15% annually. STEM fields often achieve 12-20% ROI, while liberal arts may range 5-10%. Compare to stock market average of 7-10%.

Should I include opportunity cost in ROI calculations?

Yes, opportunity cost (income foregone during school) is crucial for accurate ROI. This can add $100K-200K to total education investment depending on degree length.

How do I factor in student loan interest?

Include loan interest in total education costs. Federal loans average 4-6% interest, private loans 6-12%. This can increase total cost by 20-50% over repayment period.
