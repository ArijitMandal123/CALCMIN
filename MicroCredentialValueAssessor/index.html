Red Flags in Micro-Credentials

Unrealistic Promises

Beware of programs promising guaranteed job placement, unrealistic salary increases, or "get rich quick" outcomes. Legitimate programs provide realistic expectations and transparent outcome data.

Lack of Industry Recognition

Research whether target employers actually recognize and value the credential. Check job postings and industry forums for mentions of the specific program or provider.

Poor Cost-to-Value Ratio

Expensive programs aren't always better. Compare costs against potential salary increases and consider free or low-cost alternatives that might provide similar value.
