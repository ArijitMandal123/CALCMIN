Frequently Asked Questions

How many downloads do I need to make money from podcasting?

Most ad networks require 1,000-5,000 monthly downloads minimum. However, direct sponsorships can start with as few as 500-1,000 downloads in niche markets.

What's a good CPM rate for podcast advertising?

CPM rates vary by niche and audience quality. Business/finance podcasts can achieve $25-$50 CPM, while entertainment shows typically see $8-$15 CPM.

How accurate is this podcast revenue calculator?
